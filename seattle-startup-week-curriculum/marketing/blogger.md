## Title

How Seattle Startups Can Adopt AI-Assisted Product Engineering in One Week

## Body

Many startup teams experiment with AI tools, but few build an operational system
that scales across planning, coding, testing, and delivery.  

This curriculum was designed for a small selected group of startups in the
Greater Seattle area and follows a practical one-week path:

1. Foundations and setup  
2. Plan-first workflow discipline  
3. Memory and context control  
4. Skills and hooks automation  
5. Subagent-based parallel execution  
6. End-to-end capstone simulation  
7. Demo day and 30-day adoption roadmap

Every day includes clear outputs so teams leave with reusable practices, not
just notes.

If your startup wants practical AI engineering execution, this program provides
the structure to implement it quickly and safely.
