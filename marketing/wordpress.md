## Body

### Intro (paragraph)

The **Seattle LLM Wiki Bootcamp** site publishes a complete **one-week** facilitator and founder playbook. Teams learn to build an **Obsidian-native LLM Wiki**: raw inputs, structured pages, queryable answers, and maintenance (“lint”) — aligned with the well-known **LLM Wiki** workflow demonstrated in the program’s source video.

### What’s included (list)

- **7-Day Curriculum** — day-by-day labs and homework  
- **Discovery & Synthesis** — parallel track for messy `/raw` artifacts through affinity mapping and grounded AI assistance  
- **Graphify Track** — optional CLI path for graphs  
- **Logistics & Facilitators** — prep lists and runbooks  

### Call to action (paragraph)
